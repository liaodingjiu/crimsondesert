Skills FAQ

Q: How many skills are there?
15 skills: Kliff (5), Oongka (5), Damiane (5). Types: Active, Passive, Ultimate. Each character has 2-3 skill trees. View characters →
Q: Which character for beginners?
Kliff — Warrior tree for offense, Defender for shields. Most forgiving for new players. Oongka is high-risk, Damiane needs ranged positioning.
Q: How do skill trees work?
Unlocked via Abyss Artifacts. Mix skills across trees. Use the Skill Planner to experiment before committing in-game.
Q: What do skills cost?
Skills consume Spirit or Stamina. Spirit skills (15-80) regenerate over time. Stamina skills (20-40) drain your stamina bar. Cooldowns range from 4s (Arcane Bolt) to 90s (Berserker Rage).
