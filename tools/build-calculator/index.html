How to Use the Build Calculator

①
Pick Your Character
Select Kliff, Oongka, or Damiane from the dropdown. The equipment lists will automatically filter to show only items your character can use. Leave it on "All Characters" if you're just browsing.
②
Fill Your Gear Slots
Work through each slot — Weapon, Helmet, Chest, Gloves, Boots, Cloak, Earring, 2× Rings, Necklace. Each dropdown shows the item name, rarity, and key stats so you can compare without leaving the page.
③
Watch Stats Update Live
The right panel recalculates instantly as you equip items. Each stat shows a visual bar so you can see at a glance where your build is strong or weak. Green bars = high investment.
④
Check Set Bonuses
Equip multiple pieces from the same set and the calculator tracks your progress. A golden banner appears when you hit the required piece count — showing exactly what bonus you've unlocked.
⑤
Save & Compare Builds
Click Save to name and store your build. Saved builds appear below the stats panel — click any to load it instantly. Save multiple builds for the same character to compare different setups.
⑥
Iterate & Optimize
Swap items and watch how each change affects your totals. Ring slots are doubled (you can equip two), so they're the most flexible tuning knobs. Pair rings with Abyss Gears for fine-tuned endgame builds.
