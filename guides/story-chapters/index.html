📖 1. How Many Chapters in Crimson Desert?

The short answer: 12 main chapters, bookended by a Prologue and an Epilogue, for 14 total narrative segments. The campaign spans 168 main quests and takes roughly 35–50 hours for a focused playthrough — or 80–100+ hours for completionists.

⏱️ 2. How Long Is Crimson Desert?

Playtime varies significantly depending on your playstyle. A focused main-story run lands around 35–50 hours. Adding side quests, exploration, and optional bosses pushes it to 60–80 hours. A true 100% completionist run — legendary gear, all mounts, every Abyss Artifact — can exceed 80–100+ hours.

🗺️ 3. Chapter Progression & When to Explore

One of the most-asked questions: "How many chapters before I can explore freely?" The answer: push through to Chapter 3 or 4 before going off-script. Here's why, phase by phase.

🌅 Phase 1 — Tutorial & Setup

Prologue – Chapter 3

Introduces Kliff, the Greymanes, and core combat mechanics. Key unlocks: basic crafting, camp system, companion introduction. Stick to the main path here — the open world opens up meaningfully once you hit Chapter 4.

🗺️ Phase 2 — The World Opens Up

Chapters 4 – 6

This is when you can start exploring. Camp upgrades unlock fully, companion management comes online, and multiple regions become accessible. The game is designed around alternating between story missions and side exploration from this point forward. Recommended: finish Chapter 4, then take a break from the main quest to explore, craft, and gear up.

⚔️ Phase 3 — Mid-Game Expansion

Chapters 7 – 9

Major exploration expansion. Remaining regions unlock. Larger-scale conflicts emerge and the story takes more significant turns. Side quests become more complex and rewarding. Good time to farm gear upgrades and tackle optional bosses.

🔥 Phase 4 — Endgame

Chapters 10 – 12

Major story revelations and the hardest encounters. The Crimson Dragon and other endgame bosses demand optimized gear (Tier 5 legendary, full Abyss set). Recommended: complete all available side content and gear farming before entering Chapter 11, as the late-game difficulty spikes significantly. Some players report that certain side content becomes locked or harder to access after Chapter 11.

🏁 5. Endings, Side Quests & Completion

How Many Endings?

Crimson Desert follows a linear narrative with a single canonical ending — the Epilogue: Journey's End. Early reports suggest that while major story beats are fixed, certain character-specific outcomes and side-story conclusions may vary based on decisions made during the campaign. Full details are still being confirmed as the community reaches the endgame.

How Many Side Quests?

The exact side quest total is still being cataloged by the community. Side quests are distributed across the 5 regions of Pywel and unlock progressively with main story advancement. Most side content becomes available between Chapters 4–9, with some quests locked behind specific chapter milestones or companion relationships. Late-game (Chapter 11+) may gate or lock certain side content, so complete optional objectives before pushing into the endgame.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many chapters are in Crimson Desert?
12 main chapters, plus a Prologue (Dead of Night) and an Epilogue (Journey's End), for 14 total narrative segments. The campaign includes 168 main quests across all chapters.
Q: How long does it take to beat Crimson Desert?
A focused main-story run takes 35–50 hours. Main story + side quests and optional bosses: 60–80 hours. Full 100% completion (all legendary gear, mounts, Abyss Artifacts, every side quest): 80–100+ hours.
Q: When should I start exploring the open world?
Push the main story through Chapter 4 before going off-script. By then you'll have camp upgrades, companion management, crafting stations, and fast travel — all of which make exploration significantly more efficient. Exploring before Chapter 3 is possible but inefficient without these systems.
Q: How many endings does Crimson Desert have?
Crimson Desert has a linear narrative with one canonical ending — the Epilogue: Journey's End. While certain character-specific outcomes and side-story conclusions may vary based on campaign decisions, the core story follows a single path.
Q: Is Crimson Desert bigger than Red Dead Redemption 2?
In main story length: comparable (~35–50h vs RDR2's ~50h). In 100% completion: RDR2 is significantly larger (~180h vs 80–100h+). Crimson Desert uses a region-based world structure (5 regions of Pywel) rather than a single continuous map, with each region dense in content — closer to The Witcher 3's design philosophy.
Q: How many side quests are in Crimson Desert?
The exact side quest total is still being cataloged. Side quests are distributed across the 5 regions of Pywel, unlocking progressively with main story advancement. Most become available between Chapters 4–9. Complete optional objectives before Chapter 11, as some side content may become locked or harder to access in the late game.
