详情页 /characters/kliff/ — Kliff 角色详情页模板

⚔️
Kliff
The Shield of Pywel — Master of Sword & Shield
⚔️ 1H & 2H Swords 🪓 Axes 🗡️ Daggers 🛡️ All Armor Types
Once a captain of the fallen Hernand Guard, Kliff now wanders Pywel seeking redemption for a past he cannot undo. His blade — said to be forged from the shards of a fallen star — has never dulled, and his shield has never broken. Some say he fights not for glory, but for the ghosts that follow him.

Playstyle Overview

Kliff is the most versatile of the three characters. His three skill trees allow him to adapt to any situation — offensive sword combos (Warrior), defensive shield tactics (Defender), and party-wide buffs (Tactician). He can equip the widest range of weapons and is the best choice for new players.

Pro Tips

Tip 01

Start with Warrior tree for early game — Heavy Swing combos clear basic enemies quickly.

Tip 02

Invest in Defender before boss fights. Shield Bash can interrupt enemy ultimate attacks.

Tip 03

Pair Hwando with Insight Abyss Gears for a crit-focused build (Speed × Crit synergy).

Tip 04

Kliff's Tactician buffs affect all party members — essential for co-op boss encounters.
