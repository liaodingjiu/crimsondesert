⚔️
Kliff
The Shield of Pywel — Master of Sword & Shield
⚔️ 1H & 2H Swords 🪓 Axes 🗡️ Daggers 🛡️ All Armor Types
Once a captain of the fallen Hernand Guard, Kliff now wanders Pywel seeking redemption for a past he cannot undo. His blade — said to be forged from the shards of a fallen star — has never dulled, and his shield has never broken. Some say he fights not for glory, but for the ghosts that follow him.

Playstyle

Kliff is the most versatile of the three characters. His three skill trees allow him to adapt to any situation — offensive sword combos from the Warrior tree, defensive shield tactics from Defender, and party-wide buffs from Tactician. He can equip the widest range of weapons (1H swords, 2H swords, axes, daggers) and all armor types, making him the best choice for new players and those who want a balanced, flexible fighter.

Pro Tips

Tip 01

Start with Warrior tree for early game — Heavy Swing and Whirlwind Slash clear groups fast. Invest in Defender before your first boss fight.

Tip 02

Pair Hwando with Insight Abyss Gears for a crit-focused build. The Speed × Crit synergy on 2H swords is one of Kliff's strongest combos.

Tip 03

Shield Bash can interrupt enemy ultimate attacks. Time it right and you can cancel a boss's most dangerous moves. This alone makes Defender tree worth investing in.

Tip 04

Kliff's Tactician buffs affect all party members. In co-op, a Tactician Kliff behind a Defender Oongka is one of the most effective duos in the game.

🪓
Oongka
The Unstoppable Force — Breaker of Armies
🪓 2H Axes 🗡️ Spears 🛡️ All Armor Types
A warrior from the northern wastes where only the strong survive. Oongka never speaks of his past, but the scars on his body tell stories no words could. He wields weapons that ordinary men cannot lift, and his war cry alone has been known to route entire bandit camps. He fights not for honor — he fights because battle is the only language he knows.

Playstyle

Oongka is the heavy damage dealer. He sacrifices speed for devastating raw power — his 2H axe swings can break enemy guards and deal massive area damage. The Destroyer tree amplifies his offensive output, while Juggernaut gives him unstoppable charges and rage-fueled survivability. He has fewer skill options than Kliff, but each hit counts twice as much. Best for players who prefer slow, deliberate, punishing attacks.

Compatible Weapons

Pro Tips

Tip 01

Always charge heavy attacks. Oongka's charged swing hits multiple enemies and breaks guards. Uncharged swings waste his potential.

Tip 02

Pair Battle Axe of the Warlord with Vigor Abyss Gears. Stamina regen is critical — Oongka burns through stamina faster than any other character.

Tip 03

Juggernaut's Unstoppable Charge can plow through enemy formations. Use it to reposition or to close distance on ranged enemies.

Tip 04

Berserker Rage increases damage output but reduces defense. Activate it when you have a healer or when enemies are staggered — not when you're taking heavy fire.

Damiane
The Arcane Mystic — Weaver of Elements
🏹 Bows ✨ Arcane Magic 🔥❄️⚡ Elemental Spells 🛡️ Cloth Armor Preferred
A mystic from the eastern sanctuaries where the veil between worlds is thin. Damiane does not wield steel — she commands the elements themselves. Ice freezes her enemies in place, fire consumes them, and lightning strikes from clear skies. She seeks the Abyss Artifacts not for power, but because she believes they hold the key to sealing the Abyss forever.

Playstyle

Damiane is the ranged specialist and crowd controller. She fights from a distance with bows and devastating magic — ice to freeze, fire to burn over time, lightning to stun. The Mystic tree provides healing and arcane bolts, while the Elementalist tree unleashes elemental devastation. She has the fewest weapon options but makes up for it with spell variety. Best for tactical players who prefer controlling the battlefield over charging into it.

Pro Tips

Tip 01

Always keep distance. Damiane has the lowest defense — use Freeze spells to immobilize enemies before they close in. Frost Nova is your best panic button.

Tip 02

Elemental combos matter. Freeze a target first, then hit them with Lightning for bonus damage. Fire DoT stacks with Arcane Bolt's spirit damage.

Tip 03

Healing Light is the only healing skill in the game. In co-op, a Mystic Damiane is invaluable — the Elementalist tree does more damage, but someone needs to keep the party alive.

Tip 04

Pair Precision Compound Bow with Swift Abyss Gears. Higher attack speed means more arrows, faster spell triggers, and better DPS uptime at range.

Character FAQ

Q Which character should I pick?
Kliff — beginner-friendly, versatile, widest weapon selection. Oongka — slow but devastating, highest burst damage. Damiane — ranged specialist, best crowd control, hardest to master. New to Crimson Desert? Start with Kliff.
Q Can I switch characters mid-game?
No. You choose your character at the start. Save files are per-character. To try another, start a new game — your progress is tied to your chosen protagonist.
Q How many skill trees does each character have?
Kliff: 3 (Warrior/Defender/Tactician). Oongka: 2 (Destroyer/Juggernaut). Damiane: 2 (Mystic/Elementalist). Trees unlock via Abyss Artifacts. Use the Skill Planner to experiment.
Q Which character does the most damage?
Oongka has the highest per-hit damage with charged 2H axe attacks and Berserker Rage. Damiane has the highest sustained DPS through elemental combos. Kliff balances damage with survivability. See weapon tier list →
