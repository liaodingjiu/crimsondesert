Abyss Gear Tier List

All 5 Abyss Gear categories ranked by combat effectiveness. S-Tier for universal builds, A-Tier for specialized roles, B-Tier for utility.

⚙️ How rankings work: Categories are ranked by versatility (how many builds benefit), stat impact (how much power they add), and weapon compatibility. Individual gears within each category vary — see the Abyss Gears database for full details.
⚡
Swift
S-TIER

Movement speed and attack speed boosts. Universally useful — every build benefits from more speed. Essential for melee characters and speedrun strategies.

Best for: Kliff (melee gap-closing), all characters (mobility)
Effect: +Movement Speed, +Attack Speed, dash enhancements
Compatible with: 1H Swords, Spears, Daggers, Bows
Farm priority: Highest — get Swift II as soon as possible
🎯
Insight
S-TIER

Critical rate and critical damage amplifier. Multiplicative damage scaling — the best offensive Abyss Gear for DPS builds. Stacks with high-crit weapons like Goblin King's Treasure Dagger.

Best for: Damiane (ranged crit), Oongka (heavy burst)
Effect: +Critical Rate, +Critical Damage, detection range
Compatible with: 2H Swords, Bows, Daggers
Farm priority: Highest for DPS — pairs with Precision Compound Bow
💪
Vigor
A-TIER

Stamina regeneration and max stamina increase. Critical for shield-heavy Kliff builds and dodge-heavy playstyles. Less impactful for Damiane (ranged).

Best for: Kliff (shield/block), melee Oongka
Effect: +Stamina Regen, +Max Stamina, reduced stamina costs
Compatible with: All weapon types (universal)
Farm priority: High for melee, low for ranged
🔥
Elemental
A-TIER

Fire (Burn DoT), Ice (Freeze), Lightning (Stun). Situationally extremely powerful — essential for specific boss encounters. Ice trivializes fast bosses, Fire melts high-HP enemies.

Best for: Boss fights (type-matching), Damiane (elemental synergy)
Effect: Burn (DoT), Freeze (immobilize), Stun (interrupt)
Compatible with: Varies by weapon type and element
Farm priority: High before boss encounters — match element to boss weakness
🧘
Composure
B-TIER

Spirit regeneration and max spirit increase. Useful for skill-spam builds (Damiane Mystic, Kliff Tactician). Niche but powerful in the right setup.

Best for: Damiane (Mystic tree), Kliff (Tactician tree)
Effect: +Spirit Regen, +Max Spirit, reduced skill costs
Compatible with: All weapon types (universal)
Farm priority: Situational — only if using spirit-heavy builds
