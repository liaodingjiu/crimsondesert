Crimson Desert Armor Sets

4 complete armor sets — 17 of 20 armor pieces belong to a set. Collecting all pieces provides implicit synergy through matching defense types and Abyss Gear slot coverage.

Dark Ringleader Set
Epic · 3 Pieces

Endgame cloth/leather set. Highest DEF values in the game. Drops from the Dark Ringleader boss in Demeniss (Lv 25-40). The best armor for caster and hybrid builds.
