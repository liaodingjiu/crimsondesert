Accessories FAQ

Q: What accessories are available?
12 accessories: 4 Earrings, 4 Rings, 4 Necklaces. From Common (Iron Earring) to Legendary (Crimson Ruby Earring, Ring of the Abyss). Browse all →
Q: What's the best accessory?
Crimson Ruby Earring gives +5 Move Speed + Crit + Spirit Regen. Ring of the Abyss is the best ring. Necklace of Lightning tops necklaces.
Q: How do I get accessories?
Most are boss drops (8 of 12). Others can be crafted (Silver Ring of Precision, Necklace of Fortitude) or bought from vendors (Iron Earring, Engraved Gold Earring, Gold Necklace).
Q: Do accessories have special effects?
Yes — unique effects like Spirit Regen (Crimson Ruby Earring), Lightning damage (Necklace of Lightning), or Darkness resistance (Mark of Darkness). Each accessory lists its special effect on its detail page.
