Boss FAQ

Q: Which boss drops the best weapons?
Goyen (Fated Shadow, ATK 85) and the Abyss Warden (Abyssal Longspear, ATK 72) drop the highest-ATK weapons. The Crimson Dragon drops the best accessories. Check the Weapon Database for full drop tables.
Q: What's the recommended boss order for new players?
Goblin King (Lv 15) → Witch of the Marsh (Lv 25) → Warlord Ghost (Lv 28) → Goyen (Lv 35) → Crimson Dragon (Lv 48) → Abyss Warden (Lv 50). Gear up between each fight — don't skip tiers.
Q: Do bosses respawn?
Yes. All bosses respawn after a set cooldown period. Farm them for materials and rare drops. Legendary drops have a low drop rate — expect multiple clears to get the item you want.
Q: Can I fight bosses in co-op?
Yes. Bosses scale with party size. Co-op is recommended for Goyen and above — the extra DPS and the ability to revive teammates makes a significant difference.
