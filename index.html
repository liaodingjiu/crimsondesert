The World of Pywel

Explore 5 distinct regions — 90km² of biomes, bosses, and legendary gear

🏰
Hernand Lv 1–20

The starting region and your gateway to Pywel. Rolling grasslands, scattered villages, and ancient ruins. Rich in early-game unique weapons and the Shadow Set armor collection.

Bosses: Goblin King, Shadow Warrior Gear: Shadow Set, Hwando, Survivor's Solitude
🏔️
Pailune Lv 15–30

A mountainous region with ancient temples and harsh weather. Mid-game zone introducing complex Abyss Gears and elemental enemies. The Witch of the Marsh resides here.

Bosses: Witch of the Marsh, Warlord Ghost Gear: Tauria Curved Sword, Witch's Earring, Witch's Ring
🌑
Demeniss Lv 25–40

A dark, corrupted region where the Abyss's influence is strongest. End-game zone with legendary equipment drops. The Goyen boss encounter unlocks top-tier weapons.

Bosses: Goyen, Shadow Lord, Dark Ringleader Gear: Fated Shadow, Darkbringer, Dark Ringleader Set
🌿
Delesyia Lv 20–35

A lush but dangerous region filled with ancient forests and mythical beasts. Home to the Thunder Beast and White Lion, both dropping top-tier necklaces. Rich in alchemy and cooking ingredients.

Bosses: Thunder Beast, White Lion Gear: Necklace of Lightning, White Lion Necklace
🏜️
Crimson Desert Lv 35–50

The namesake endgame region. A harsh desert wasteland with the game's toughest challenges. The Abyss Warden and Crimson Dragon await — dropping some of the best gear in the game.

Bosses: Abyss Warden, Crimson Dragon Gear: Abyssal Longspear, Crimson Ruby Earring, Ring of the Abyss

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Crimson Desert's mechanics, characters, and systems

Q What is Crimson Desert?
Crimson Desert is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Pearl Abyss, set in the continent of Pywel. It features free-form combat, a non-linear story, and a seamless 90km² world to explore.
Q Is Crimson Desert an MMO?
No. Crimson Desert is primarily a single-player open-world game, with optional online elements. It shares the same engine (BlackSpace) as Black Desert Online but is a separate, narrative-driven experience.
Q How many playable characters are there?
Three — Kliff (sword & shield warrior), Oongka (heavy weapons destroyer), and Damiane (arcane mystic). Each has unique skill trees and combat styles. Browse all skills →
Q How do I compare weapons and armor?
Use the Equipment Compare tool to see stats side-by-side, or the Build Calculator to equip a full gear set and see total stats for each character. Filter by rarity, type, and character for accurate comparisons.
Q How are weapon tier rankings calculated?
Composite Score = ATK + (Speed × 3) + (Crit × 2). Speed and Crit are weighted because they impact DPS more than raw ATK. All rankings are data-driven — no opinion, just math. See the full tier list →
Q What are Abyss Gears?
Socketable items that add special attacks and effects to your weapons and armor. They are the game's most unique customization system — different Gears are compatible with different weapon types. Categories include Swift, Insight, Vigor, Composure, and Elemental. Browse Abyss Gears →
Q How big is the map?
The continent of Pywel spans approximately 90km² across 5 distinct regions — Hernand, Pailune, Demeniss, Delesyia, and the Crimson Desert. Each region has unique biomes, enemies, bosses, and gear drops.
Q How do I find where a specific item drops?
Every equipment page has an Acquisition section showing how to get it — boss drop (with boss name and region), location find, crafting recipe, or vendor purchase. Browse Weapons, Armor, or Accessories and click any item for full details.
