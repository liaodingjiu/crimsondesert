Frequently Asked Questions

Q How many weapons are in Crimson Desert?
20 weapons across 6 types: one-handed swords (7), two-handed swords (5), spears (3), axes (2), daggers (2), and bows (2). Rarities range from Uncommon to Legendary. Browse all weapons →
Q What are the armor sets in Crimson Desert?
4 main armor sets: Shadow Set (5 plate pieces), Fallen Kingdom Set (5 plate/leather pieces), Dark Ringleader Set (3 cloth/leather pieces), and Iron Plate Set (4 craftable pieces). Browse armor sets →
Q How do I get legendary equipment?
Legendary equipment drops from endgame bosses: Goyen (3 legendaries in Demeniss), Abyss Warden (2 legendaries in Crimson Desert), and Crimson Dragon (1 legendary earring). All require Lv 32-40. See weapon tier list →
Q What's the best early game equipment?
For Lv 1-15: Iron Plate Set (craftable, solid DEF), Iron Sword (craftable, Lv 5), Hunter's Bow (Damiane), Scout Leather Vest (vendor). See full early game guide →
Q Can equipment be upgraded?
No traditional upgrade system. Progression comes from finding higher-tier equipment. Abyss Gears can be socketed into weapons and armor to add special effects and skills. Browse Abyss Gears →
Q How do I compare equipment?
Use our Equipment Compare tool to see stats side-by-side. The Build Calculator lets you equip a full set and see total stats for each character.
