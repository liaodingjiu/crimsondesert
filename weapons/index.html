Weapon FAQ

Q: What is the best weapon in Crimson Desert?
For raw Attack, Abyssal Longspear (ATK 56) leads. Fated Shadow (ATK 55) and Darkbringer (ATK 39, Crit 3) are top legendary picks. See the Weapon Tier List →
Q: Can any character use any weapon?
No. Kliff uses swords, spears, daggers. Oongka uses spears and axes. Damiane uses bows. Check the compatibility table →
Q: 1H vs 2H — which is better?
1H = lower ATK, faster swings. 2H = higher damage, slower. Choose based on playstyle — fast combos vs heavy hits.
Q: How do I get legendary weapons?
Drops from endgame bosses: Goyen (3 legendaries), Abyss Warden (Abyssal Longspear). Most are in Demeniss and Crimson Desert regions.
